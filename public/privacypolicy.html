Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Mr. Speedy Development. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data when you use our Roblox games and services.

By using our games and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. This policy applies to all games developed by Mr. Speedy Development on the Roblox platform.

Information We Collect

Roblox User Information

When you play our games, we may collect the following information through Roblox's platform:

  • Your Roblox username and User ID
  • Your avatar appearance and accessories
  • Your friends list (if you choose to share it)
  • Your gameplay statistics and progress
  • Your in-game purchases and virtual currency transactions

Gameplay Data

We collect information about your gameplay to improve your experience:

  • Game progress and achievements
  • Time spent in games
  • Server performance and crash reports
  • Chat messages (moderated by Roblox)
  • Player interactions and social features usage

Technical Information

We may collect technical information to ensure our games run smoothly:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Internet connection information
  • Game performance metrics
  • Error logs and debugging information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Operation: To provide, maintain, and improve our games
  • Player Support: To respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Game Development: To analyze gameplay patterns and develop new features
  • Security: To detect and prevent cheating, harassment, and other violations
  • Communication: To send important updates about our games (through Roblox)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it:

  • Data is stored on secure servers with encryption
  • Access to personal information is limited to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review and update our security practices
  • We use Roblox's secure platform infrastructure
  • We conduct regular security audits and monitoring

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data using industry-standard practices.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Roblox Corporation: As required by the Roblox platform terms of service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist in operating our games (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Safety Concerns: To address safety issues or protect the rights of our users

Any sharing of information is done in accordance with applicable privacy laws and with appropriate safeguards in place.

Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable laws:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • We rely on Roblox's age verification and parental consent systems
  • Children's accounts have additional privacy protections through Roblox
  • Parents can review and delete their child's information through their Roblox account
  • We provide age-appropriate experiences and content

If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal information
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection: You can object to certain uses of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. Note that some requests may be subject to Roblox's own privacy controls and policies.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Gameplay data is retained while you actively play our games
  • Account information is retained as long as your Roblox account exists
  • Technical logs are typically retained for 90 days
  • Support communications are retained for 2 years
  • Legal compliance data is retained as required by law

When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention policies.

Cookies and Tracking

Our games operate within the Roblox platform, which may use cookies and similar technologies:

  • We do not directly set cookies in our games
  • Roblox may use cookies for platform functionality
  • We may use analytics tools to understand game performance
  • You can control cookie settings through your browser or Roblox account

For more information about Roblox's use of cookies, please refer to Roblox's Privacy Policy.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements:

  • We will notify you of significant changes through our games or website
  • The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will be revised
  • Continued use of our games after changes constitutes acceptance
  • We encourage you to review this policy periodically
